Privacy Policy

LAST UPDATED: JUNE 2026

1. Information We Collect

At ZEUS IOT, we only collect the information necessary to fulfill your hardware and software requests. This includes your name, email address, WhatsApp number, college/university name, and any specific project requirements or documents you submit through our contact, learning, or report generation forms.

2. How We Use Your Data

Your data is used strictly for communication regarding your project requests, delivery logistics, and post-delivery support. We use your WhatsApp number to send you structured updates, project reports, and payment verification confirmations. We do not use your information for marketing spam.

3. Data Security & Storage

We take security seriously. Any files uploaded to our servers (such as syllabus PDFs, architecture diagrams, or previous code) are stored securely in our database and filesystem. Your data is not publicly accessible and is only reviewed by the engineers directly working on your project.

4. Third-Party Sharing

ZEUS IOT does not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. We may share necessary delivery information (like address and phone number) exclusively with our trusted shipping/courier partners to ensure your IoT hardware reaches you safely.

5. Code & Intellectual Property Confidentiality

We understand that many of our clients are working on unique innovations. Any custom ideas, algorithms, or requirements you share with us remain confidential. The final delivered software and hardware schematics are provided exclusively for your use.

6. Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns regarding our privacy practices or wish to have your data removed from our systems after your project is completed, please reach out to us at zeusiotprojects@gmail.com or via our WhatsApp support line.
